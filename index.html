Oakland
Map Library

Libraries collect and steward community knowledge. They serve as an archive and a resource, designed to inform, engage, and be of use to their patrons. The Oakland Map Library documents the landscape of contemporary mapping while making these works visible and accessible. It is a collection of interactive maps, story maps, map art and more that spans themes of equity, environment, and community.
